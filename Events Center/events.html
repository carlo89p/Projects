Upcoming Events

We are pleased to confirm that our facility maintains an operational status throughout the entire year, catering to a diverse schedule that adapts to every season. This commitment means that regardless of the month, the track is active with activities ranging from major professional series to essential testing and development sessions. Our primary goal is to ensure continuous access to world-class racing and related driving experiences for both participants and spectators.

The yearly calendar is structured around three main phases. The spring and fall months host our most intensive schedule, featuring championship races, international qualifiers, and large public festivals. The peak summer period is often utilized for specialized high-performance driving academies, corporate rentals, and vehicle media launches. While the winter schedule sees fewer major spectator events, this time is crucial for private testing, facility maintenance, and local club track days, ensuring the venue is constantly being utilized and prepared for the busy seasons ahead.

Our events programming is consciously varied to appeal to the widest possible audience. We host everything from the pinnacle of endurance racing to grassroots amateur competitions, motocross showcases, and non-motorsport public gatherings. This variety ensures that every visit to the events center offers a unique and engaging experience, reflecting the broad scope of vehicle performance and engineering.
