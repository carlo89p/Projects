Welcome to the F1 events center

This aerial perspective presents our comprehensive motorsports complex, which is designed to host a variety of racing disciplines and public events. Conveniently located a short distance from the coast, the facility offers easy access for both regional and out-of-state visitors arriving via major highways or nearby international airports. We are situated close enough to the amenities of Virginia Beach to provide diverse accommodations and entertainment options for event attendees.

The course itself incorporates a total length of 3.2 kilometers, featuring a specific configuration that balances speed and technical driving requirements. Key track elements include three distinct braking zones and a significant elevation change of approximately 18 meters across the lap. These features are intended to test vehicle setup and driver capability consistently throughout any race distance.

The facility is equipped with dedicated support areas essential for hosting major events. These include a permanent medical center, a multi-bay technical inspection area, and capacity for over 150 team transporters in the paddock. The spectator areas offer multiple vantage points, with main grandstands providing seating for 15,000 and accessible viewing mounds along several corners.
