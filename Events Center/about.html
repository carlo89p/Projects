Our Track's History

Our events center was first conceived in the late 1990s and officially broke ground in 2001. The original layout was designed by an acclaimed firm specializing in Formula One circuit development, establishing an immediate benchmark for quality and challenge in North America. The venue quickly became a preferred location for vehicle manufacturers and professional teams seeking a versatile testing environment.

Since its opening, the facility has undergone two major renovations to enhance spectator viewing and update pit lane infrastructure. We have continuously adapted to meet the evolving demands of modern motorsport, while remaining a fixture in the regional community. Today, it stands as a premier destination known for its high-caliber events and operational excellence.

Disclaimer notice:

Our facility maintains a permanent, fully equipped medical center and highly trained emergency response teams on site during all hot track sessions. We constantly review and update protocols to ensure the safest possible environment for everyone entering the grounds.
